Jacek Jędrasik aka Yen Droll
Composer / Producer / Sound Designer


I’m a music composer at heart, with a background in psychology. I write music for theater, animations, short films, and commercial projects.

Over the years, my work has been performed on some of the most respected stages in Warsaw, such as T. Studio, T. Nowy, and T. Powszechny, as well as internationally at venues including the Baryshnikov Center in New York, Sadler’s Wells in London, and The State Theatre of Nations in Moscow (before 2022). Performances featuring my music have also travelled to Milan, Berlin, Vienna, Bucharest, Belgrad, Thessaloniki, Tallin, Vilnius, Erevan, Tbilisi, and even as far as Bali, Indonesia.

I’ve also been part of several alternative bands. Most notably is Projekt Warszawiak. Where I found a lot of joy in the freedom, rough edges, and energy of making art for underground audiences.

As a live-act and concert performer, I regularly take part in Polish art festivals, where I can bring my music into direct contact with the audience. In recent years, my work has moved between literature, sound, and audiovisual forms. I’m inspired by surrealism, psychedelia, ethnic and classical music. And of course by silence.
