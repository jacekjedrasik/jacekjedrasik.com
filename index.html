Jacek Jędrasik


I am a music composer at heart and a psychologist by education. I create music for theater plays, animations, and short films. My works have been performed on some of the most prestigious stages in Warsaw, including T. Studio, T. Nowy, and T. Powszechny, as well as internationally at venues such as the Baryshnikov Center (New York), Sadler’s Wells (London), and The State Theatre of Nations (Moscow). By profession, I am a psychologist.

As a member of several well-regarded alternative bands, most notably Projekt Warszawiak, I embraced the creative freedom that comes with making art for underground audiences.

As a live-act and concert performer, I take great satisfaction in participating in acclaimed Polish art festivals.

In recent years, my work has bridged the realms of literature and audiovisual arts. I draw inspiration from surrealism, psychedelia, ethnic music, classical music, and… silence.
